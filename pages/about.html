About Us

Who We Are

Sweitzer Technologies LLC delivers high-quality, innovative IT solutions to businesses and individuals in Southwest Missouri. We specialize in managed IT, cybersecurity, cloud solutions, and break-fix services. Our mission is to simplify technology, allowing our clients to focus on what truly matters.

What We Do

We offer fully managed IT services for small and medium-sized businesses, including proactive monitoring, remote and on-site support, cybersecurity, and cloud solutions. Our goal is to keep your technology secure, efficient, and cost-effective.

The Advantages of a Decentralized MSP

Unlike traditional IT firms, Sweitzer Technologies operates as a decentralized Managed Service Provider (MSP). This allows us to provide faster response times, lower operational costs, and greater flexibility to meet client needs. We offer remote support while ensuring dedicated on-site service when required.

Our History

Founded in 2019 as "Eazy Tek" by Joseph Sweitzer, the company began as a dropshipping business before evolving into a local IT repair service. In 2020, it started transitioning into an MSP model, but the COVID-19 pandemic led to its temporary closure.

In July 2023, Joseph re-established the company in Aurora, Missouri, incorporating it as Sweitzer Technologies LLC. After working full-time in IT throughout 2023 and 2024, he fully committed to growing the business in 2025, making it a leading MSP in Southwest Missouri.

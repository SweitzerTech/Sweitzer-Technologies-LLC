Sweitzer Technologies LLC - Remote MSP Startup Offering

Overview

Sweitzer Technologies LLC introduces a low-cost, remote-only Managed IT Services (MSP) solution designed for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) seeking full IT support, website management, inventory systems, and strategic business continuity planning. This model ensures that businesses receive comprehensive IT support without the high costs of traditional MSP services.

Service Offering

  • Remote IT Support - 24/7 monitoring, helpdesk support, proactive maintenance.
  • Cloud-Based IT Systems & Cybersecurity - Office 365, Google Workspace, cloud backups, endpoint protection.
  • Website & Inventory Management Support - Website design, SEO, e-commerce integration, POS system automation.
  • Business Technology & Continuity Meetings - Monthly strategy sessions, disaster recovery planning, software recommendations.

Target Market

Competitive Advantage

  • Lower cost than traditional MSPs with a 100% remote model, reducing operational expenses.
  • Bundled IT, website, and inventory management in one package, providing more value than standalone IT support services.

  • Scalable pricing plans to support business growth without high upfront costs.
